# Dr. Ferdi Overview

## Summary
Dr. Ferdi Iskandar is the Founder, Chief Executive Officer, and Clinical Steward of Sentra Healthcare Solutions. He combines medical training, hospital executive leadership, civil law expertise, and deep technical vision to address systemic failures in healthcare with governance-first design.

## Key Facts
- Licensed physician under Indonesia's medical licensing authority.
- 12+ years active in clinical and healthcare executive environments.
- Chief Executive Officer of a private national hospital for over 9 years.
- Led the hospital through COVID-19 while maintaining operational continuity.
- Achieved measurable operational improvements, including lower infection rates, lower readmissions, fewer medical errors, and stronger margins.
- Qualified civil law specialist with medical malpractice expertise.
- Reviewed and analyzed 140+ malpractice cases in Indonesia (2020–2025).
- Active researcher in responsible AI for healthcare.
- Research and leadership work has been cited by WHO, academic journals, and government policy documents.

## Core Identity
He is not positioned as a technologist entering healthcare or a physician dabbling in software. His profile is that of a practicing healthcare executive who designed infrastructure from first principles, with patient safety, accountability, and governance as foundational constraints.

## Mission
His mission is to improve healthcare safety, quality, and accessibility through responsible Human–AI collaboration, with humans remaining accountable for all clinical outcomes.
